ABOUT ME

I AM A ARTIST & PSYCHOTHERAPIST

At First
I earned my JD at Stanford Law School in 1976 when I was 32. After practicing law for 20 years, in San Francisco, Washington, DC and New York, I took a different path. In 1998, at 54, I graduated from Smith College School for Social Work and took up practice as a psychotherapist. I joyfully maintain a full-time private practice, in Dupont Circle, Washington, DC, specializing in the treatment of trauma-based disorders.
